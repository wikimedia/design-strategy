Initiatives

Online Social Behavior

Examining the prosocial and disruptive dimensions of online culture - on and off-wiki

Overview

This initiative focuses on understanding a wide range of social behaviors on the Wikimedia projects and on other digital platforms, with the goal of identifying and amplifying patterns of healthy engagement and of tracking and mitigating socially destructive norms, practices and policies. The studies here include original ethnographic work and quantitative studies as well as literature reviews of prior industry and academic research.

Studies

  1. HabLatAm
     

    In progress A cross country study on the internet skills, habits, and behaviors of youth in Latin America.

  2. Taxonomy of Harrassment

    Jan 2021 A taxonomy of negative social interactions on Wiki projects and a methodology to prioritize those in need of intervention.

  3. CheckUser Improvements

    Oct 2020 Past research and Steward feedback surfaced many pain points in the CheckUser extension, the tool used by moderators to reverse vandalism of articles. These are multiple iterative rounds of usability tests of the enhanced tool.

  4. Patrolling Anonymous Edits

    Oct 2020 A study of how patrollers handle edits by logged-out users, and what pieces of information from IP addresses are necessary for their work in maintaining the overall health and quality of Wikimedia projects.

  5. Content Moderation Explained

    June 2020 An overview of how a global community of volunteer moderators determine which contributions to Wikipedia are accepted and rejected.

  6. Interpersonal Communication on Wikipedia

    June 2020 A review of the available academic literature on interpersonal communication and coordination within the Wikipedia community, in particular the role of talk pages in creating and maintaining Wikipediaâ€™s content.

  7. Community Health Survey

    Sep 2019 An inquiry into the unique challenges faced by volunteer administrators in conflict resolution situations and what types of resources would be most beneficial to this work.

  8. IP Masking Impact Report

    July 2019 An examination of how edits by users who have not logged-in effect administrator workflows.

  9. Reporting System Rubric for the Community Health Initiative

    Feb 2019 A review of best practices in reporting systems for disputes and abuse across different platforms.

  10. Harassment on Arabic Wikipedia

    Feb 2019 This study aimed to establish a baseline understanding of the frequency of harassment, trolling, and threatening communicative practices on Arabic Wikipedia, to explore the effects of these practices on editing and community health, and to guide future investigation and strategic decisions for the Anti-Harassment Tools team.

  11. Steward Spambot Workflow

    Dec 2018 An investigation into one of the most common tasks performed by stewards, global locks of suspected spambots that are active across multiple wikis.
